About

I see myself as a designer and a creative engineer. As a designer, I have a profound interest in human-centred design and user experience research. As an engineer I enjoy problem-solving and to find smart and feasible solutions.

I like to work in situations where user-centric design and technology have a central role and where I can be part of the digital development.

Learn more about me.

A Tribute to Gelato

To learn more about web design and web development I signed up for a distance course called Dynamic Web Applications from Uppsala University right before the summer year 2019. After the course I created a website to celebrate gelato.
