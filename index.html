WHAT IS GELATO?

Gelato is a popular frozen dessert of Italian origin. Gelato typically has 50% less fat than traditional ice cream, but it tastes much creamier. The creamy texture comes from the way it’s made. Produced in small batches, slow churning instead of rapid beating. This produces a denser product served at -15 instead of -18,
