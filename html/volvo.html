Role Description

I currently work as a Human Machine Interface Engineer in an agile development team implementing end-to-end functions for our in-house Android entertainment system. In my current role, it is important to be multilingual. Not in the sense of speaking multiple languages, but in the sense of effectively communicate with a wide range of stakeholders. I collaborate with UX designers to create view-flows for the functions that I am responsible for.

In this role, it is important to coordinate the flow of information and to be organized in order to work successfully.
